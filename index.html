About Us

Problem

Thousands of Families and families in India are unable to get sufficient foods and they sleep on empty stomach.

india is a country where there are lots of farmers which produce surplus agri-products for our peoples. and then in a country like india many people sleep empty stomach. So , we can have a solution as we have resources.

In this World, about 1/3rd food gets wasted which is a really big issues. So we can make a portal which can help those needie peoples.

Solution

A Web Platform on which peoples who have maked surplus food that may be get spoiled, or is going to get spoiled can make a request for a pickup so that our volunteers will pickup the food from them and distributes in the poors.

By this platform organizers, hotel-management, homes where the food may get wasted can make a request so that our volunteers will pick up the requested food and will distribute the foods.
